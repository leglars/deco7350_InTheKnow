14 October 2015

US basketball and reality TV star Lamar Odom is fighting for life in hospital after he was found unconscious at a brothel.

NYE County Sheriff Sharon A Wehrly says the department got an emergency call on Tuesday for an unresponsive man at the Love Ranch in Crystal, Nevada about 112km outside of Las Vegas.

The sheriff's statement says Odom was stabilised and taken to Desert View Hospital in nearby Pahrump.

Paramedics wanted to airlift him to a Las Vegas hospital, but he was too tall for the helicopter and was driven by ambulance.

There was no further word on his condition.

Sources told TMZ.com that Odom arrived at the infamous brothel on Saturday and had been partying with women "for days" before he was found.

Brothel owner Dennis Hof told TheWrap.com that Odom's condition was "not good".

Odom, 35, retired from the NBA in 2013 and was featured on reality TV during his four-year marriage to Khloe Kardashian.

Kardashian filed for divorce in December, 2013.

The former Los Angeles Lakers player has battled substance abuse issues in the past.
